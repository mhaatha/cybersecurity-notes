HISTORY OF CYBERSECURITY
- Creeper and Reaper is the first computer worms (1970s).
- Morris Worm (1980s).
- Script Kiddies (1990s).
- Cybercrime starts to attack companies (2000s).
- Attacks by nation-states increased in frequency (2010s).
- WannaCry and NotPetya (2017).

Hacking is the act of working within the confines of a system to produce unintended behaviour.
